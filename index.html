About Us

A Family Business
Built on Integrity

Wunsch Construction Inc. has been a cornerstone of the Greene, Iowa community since 1990. Family-owned and operated, we've built our reputation on honest work, reliable crews, and results that stand the test of time.

With a tight-knit team of 10–20 experienced professionals and our own fleet of equipment, we handle projects of all sizes — from residential builds to large-scale commercial and underground infrastructure work.
