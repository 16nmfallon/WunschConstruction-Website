About Us

A Family Business Built on Integrity

Wunsch Construction Inc. has been a cornerstone of the Greene, Iowa community since its founding on March 8, 1990. A family-owned and operated company, we've built our reputation on honest work, reliable crews, and results that stand the test of time.

With a fleet of equipment and a tight-knit team of 10 to 20 experienced professionals, we handle projects of all sizes — from residential builds to large-scale commercial and underground infrastructure work. When you hire Wunsch, you're hiring neighbors who take pride in what they build.

What We Do

Our Services

From foundations to finished structures, Wunsch Construction handles a full range of general contracting and underground work across Iowa.

General Contracting

Full-service general contracting for residential and commercial projects. We manage every phase from planning to final walkthrough.

Residential Construction

New home builds and residential structures. We specialize in multi-unit residential buildings and custom construction throughout Butler County.

Underground & Sewer Construction

Experienced underground contractors with a long history in sewer line installation, underground utilities, and infrastructure work.

Material Hauling & Transport

Intrastate hauling of construction materials and large equipment across Iowa. Our fleet of 4 power units keeps your project on schedule.

Commercial Construction

Commercial building projects handled with the same care and craftsmanship as our residential work. Serving businesses across north-central Iowa.

Site Work & Excavation

Site preparation, grading, and excavation services to get your project started on solid footing. We work with aggregates and handle large-scale earthwork.
